
## Developers Certificate Of Origin
If you're contributing, please make sure you *sign your commits!*
And keep your commit messages clear.


## Code Style
Code added to the repository should be structured, long tuples/arrays/dictionaries should have each
entry on a newline. Short ones may have entries on the same line.



## Legal Threats and Licensing Discussions

Do not use legal threats, intimidating legal language, or hostile wording in issues or pull requests.

If you believe a fork or contribution has a licensing or attribution problem, please raise it politely and factually.  
We will resolve licensing concerns calmly and respectfully.

Legal intimidation, aggressive wording, or threatening actions are not tolerated.
